Place Where is it? What is it?
San José It's right in the center of Costa Rica A big city with many fun things to do, such as museums and different bars and restraunts.
Parque Nacional Manuel Antonio South of San José, on the southwest coast. A national park with rainforests and beaches, and sloth protection sanctuaries.
Monteverde Northern Costa Rica, in a rainforest. Hiking trails through a tropical rainforest with tour groups. There are even wood/rope bridges over rivers and streams in the forests.
