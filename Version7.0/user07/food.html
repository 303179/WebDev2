Foods To Eat




Some of the most popular foods to eat in Costa Rica





Gallo Pinto
  1. Can be found and eaten from booths on the street
  2. Most popular and enjoyed by many local people
  3. Made of Rice & Beans
Arroz con Leche
  1. Rice with Cinnamon and Cream/Milk
  2. My dad makes this every year for Christmas and it's delicious!
  3. Very common as a dessert or snack in Costa Rica
Casado
  1. This dish can be found in many restraunts all around Costa Rica
  2. Made with rice, meat, and beans primarily
